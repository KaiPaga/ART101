Hi and welcome new users to the story of previous long distance runner and current udnergraduate student, Kai Pagan. Orginally born on the island of Oahu and raised their until the age of 15, Kai spent a majority of his early teen years and childhood barbecuing on sandy beaches and eating spam musbis from local seven elevns for lunch. Kai also did a little bit of horse back riding, as he used to take beginneer lessons, in the sport and while I never liked institutionalized basketball loved to play the street verison of it with friends.

After moving to the East Bay of California, Kai finished highschool at at Newark Memorial Highschool, where for his last year, joined his school cross country and track and field team. While, Kai never placed first in any of his races, the socail connections he made and atheletic preservance that he would eventually come to show were more than enough for him.

Kai attended Ohlone communitty college, received an associates degree in Sociology, decided to take 2 years off from school to travel and solely focus on work for a bit, and then transferred over to the Unveristy of California, Santa Cruz. Kai is a third-year, sociology major, with a concentration in Global Information and Social Enterprise Studies.
