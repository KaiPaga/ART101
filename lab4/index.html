    How to Hem a Pair of Pants
  1. Remove the Pre-existing Hem If There is One.
  2. Fold the bottom of the pants inward until they are the desired length.
  3. Pin the Fabric Above the Fold.
  4. Iron the Fold so That It Is Straight.
  5. Using a Sewing Machine, Sew a New Line Above the Fold.
  6. Cut the Excess Fabric Above the New Hem Off.
  7. Turn Your Freshly Hemmed pANTS right-Side Out
